About us

Technology that puts hospitality first

Monk Food helps restaurants, takeaways and bars take orders their way — and keep more of what they earn.

Our story

Built for owners, not middlemen

Monk Food started with a simple frustration: hospitality businesses were being sold technology and then left to find customers on their own. We believed there was a better way — one where a single partner gives you the tools and actively drives the sales.

So we built a single platform that brings online ordering, EPOS and QR code ordering together — and we didn't stop at technology. We roll up our sleeves on marketing too, actively bringing customers and orders to our venues. We don't just give you the tech; we bring the sales.

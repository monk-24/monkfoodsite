How it works

From scan to table in four easy steps

1

Scan the QR code

The guest scans the code on their table — your menu opens instantly in their browser, no app to download.

2

Order & pay

They browse the menu, add items and pay securely from their seat — or start a running tab.

3

Receipt hits the kitchen

The order prints or appears on the kitchen screen with the table number attached — ready to prepare.

4

Food to the table

Your team plates it and runs it straight to the guest — no queues, no missed or wrong orders.

Faster service, bigger baskets

When customers can order another round of drinks instantly, they do. Average order values jump significantly with QR ordering.

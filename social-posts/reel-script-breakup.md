# Monk Food — Viral Reel Script
## "Breaking up with your delivery app" 💔

**Format:** 9:16 vertical (film phone sideways-up / vertical) · **Length:** ~28–32s · **Cast:** 2 people (Owner + "Delivery App") · **Location:** an empty restaurant table

---

### The idea
A restaurant owner sits across from a person representing "the delivery app" — like a bad relationship break-up. It's dramatic, funny, and every hospitality owner will feel it. Ends on Monk Food = the glow-up.

### What you need
- 2 people: **Owner** (you or staff) + **App** (wears a plain delivery-style jacket/backpack, or just a lanyard that says "Delivery App")
- A restaurant table, 2 chairs, 2 coffees
- A phone on a tripod (or propped up) filming vertical
- Good light (sit near a window)

---

## SHOT-BY-SHOT

**SCENE 1 — The set-up (0:00–0:04)** 🎬
- **Shot:** Both sat at the table, tense. Owner slides a coffee across.
- **On-screen text (top):** `POV: breaking up with your delivery app`
- **Owner (serious):** "We need to talk."
- **App (nervous):** "…is this about the commission?"

**SCENE 2 — The roast (0:04–0:14)** 🔥
- **Shot:** Tighter on Owner, frustrated, counting on fingers.
- **Owner:** "You take 14% of *every* order. You keep *my* customers. You keep *my* data. And what do I get?"
- **App (small voice):** "…brand exposure?"
- **Owner (deadpan to camera):** "Brand exposure." 🙄
- **On-screen text:** `every. single. order.`

**SCENE 3 — The line (0:14–0:19)** 💅
- **Shot:** Owner stands up, grabs their coat.
- **Owner:** "I found someone who actually brings me sales. Not a bill."
- **App:** "Who?!"
- **Owner (turning back, half-smile):** "Monk Food."
- **SFX/beat drop here** (add trending audio hit)

**SCENE 4 — The glow-up (0:19–0:26)** ✨
- **Shot:** Quick cuts — Owner behind the counter, phone pinging with direct orders, tapping a Monk Food EPOS screen, a customer scanning a QR at a table, food going out.
- **On-screen text (one per cut):** `own your customers` → `keep your margins` → `they bring the sales`
- No dialogue — let the music carry it.

**SCENE 5 — The button (0:26–0:30)** ⬛
- **Shot:** Cut to black card.
- **On-screen text:** **MONK FOOD** / "we bring the sales, not the bill" / **monkfood.co.uk · Book a demo**
- *(You can use the end-card from your reel — I can export just that clip.)*

## AUDIO
Use a **trending "confrontation / break-up" or dramatic beat-drop sound** — search Reels/TikTok audio for whatever's trending that week with a hard drop around the 14–19s mark (lands on "Monk Food"). Trending audio = far more reach.

## FILMING TIPS
- Film each scene 2–3 times, pick the funniest take.
- Keep phone **vertical**, subject in the middle third (captions sit top & bottom).
- Overact slightly — comedy reels reward big expressions.
- Total edit: trim to ~28s, hard cuts on the beat.

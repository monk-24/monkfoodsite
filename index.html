How QR ordering works

From scan to kitchen in seconds

Turn every table into a point of sale without adding staff.

  • 1

    Guest scans the QR code

    No app download — the menu opens instantly in their browser.

  • 2

    They order & pay at the table

    Full menu, images, modifiers and secure card or wallet payment.

  • 3

    Order fires to your kitchen

    Tickets print or hit the kitchen screen with table number attached.

  • 4

    You track everything live

    Sales, tables and stock update in real time across all channels.
